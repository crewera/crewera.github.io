Oligmed is a technology-intensive bio-startup
developing ingredients for FSMP and Nutraceuticals
based on Industry-Academic-Research collaboration.

Founded in 2025, Oligmed develops raw materials
based on edible & medicinal natural products
to target appetite regulation, muscle function, pain relief, and cognitive disorders.
